Top 3 Favorite Tom & Jerry Characters

Image Info Characteristics

Tom

Tom is a gray and white domestic shorthair cat who usually tries to catch Jerry the mouse. Known for his clever, yet often unsuccessful attempts, Tom frequently engages in slapstick comedy chases with his nemesis.
  • Determined
  • Competitive
  • Persistent

Jerry

Jerry is a small brown mouse who is constantly pursued by Tom. Clever and resourceful, Jerry often outsmarts Tom with his quick thinking, escaping capture time and again.
  • Witty
  • Resourceful
  • Playful

Spike

Spike is a muscular bulldog with a tough exterior. Despite his gruff personality, he cares deeply for his son Tyke and often gets involved in Tom and Jerry’s antics, especially when Tom annoys him.
  • Protective
  • Strong
  • Loyal

Butch

Butch is a tough black cat and one of Tom's rivals, often trying to outdo Tom in catching Jerry. He is street-smart and occasionally competes with Tom over the affection of the female cat, Toodles Galore.
  • Confident
  • Jealous
  • Aggressive

Tyke

Tyke is Spike’s adorable and innocent puppy. He is often seen with his father and is the innocent target of Tom’s antics. Tyke is a sweet and playful pup who gets along with everyone.
  • Adorable
  • Innocent
  • Playful
